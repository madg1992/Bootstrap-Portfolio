About Me

Hello World! My name is Mark Gallardo. Currently, I am student at the Southern Methodist University Coding Bootcamp. I decided to part take on this new and challenging journey mainly because I wanted a better career - a career that I will actually enjoy doing. In my circle of friends, I've always been known as the creative mind which made me their go-to person when for creating t-shirt designs, to invitations and even brand logos. I enjoy making things from scratch and using my creativity to develop something that I can be proud of. Coding seems to be the perfect fit for me because the possibilitites and pathways are endless and abundant. To creating software or programs that will help simplify the lives of people, to Artificial Intelligence, and even game design - the future of coding is just beginning. Programming is a field that pushes you to be creative and to constantly learn and develop new skills.
